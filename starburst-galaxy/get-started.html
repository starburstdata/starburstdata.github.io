    Get started with Starburst Galaxy #

    New Starburst Galaxy account #

    If your organization is new to Starburst Galaxy, you need to request access. Visit the URL you receive and provide your email address as username.

    You receive an activation code in your email shortly after. Use the code to create your account and supply a domain for your account.

    Now you can login at the domain. Create a password for your account, which also acts as administrator.

    With this account you can proceed to create catalogs, clusters, or perform administrative tasks such as creating further users.

    Video: See it all in action with the quick walkthrough of Starburst Galaxy or the get started demoget started video.

    New Starburst Galaxy user #

    If your organization already uses Starburst Galaxy and therefore has an account, contact your account administrator to create a user with your email address.

Privacera and Starburst - Enabling secure queries and consistent governance and compliance

Hosts: Claudius Li and Bill Brooks
Video date: 20 January 2021
Running time: 35min


Presented by Bill Brooks, Director of Solutions Engineering at Privacera, and Claudius Li, Product Manager for engine and security products at Starburst. This webinar demonstrates how Privacera and Starburst integrate to to build an enterprise-ready, elastic infrastructure at scale. This session covers the following topics:

  • Starburst overview of compliance, governance, and secure queries
  • Starburst demo
  • Privacera overview
  • Privacera demo
